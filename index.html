The ear behind the board.

Luca Pretolesi has spent twenty-five years and more than thirty thousand studio hours chasing one thing: the moment a record stops sounding mixed and starts sounding inevitable. Born in Genova. Built in Las Vegas. Trusted from São Paulo to Seoul.

DMI — Digital Music Innovation — pairs a hybrid analog/digital chain with a relentlessly modern ear. The result is a signature: loud without fatigue, wide without blur, low end you feel in your sternum.

05The room

True in here. True everywhere.

Behind two isolation doors, the control room holds a chain refined over a decade: an SPL analog mastering console, mastering-grade conversion, and a calibrated 7.1.4 immersive array — inside acoustics treated to within ±1.5 dB. What you approve here is what the world hears, on every system it owns.
